Turn Every Employee Into a Digital Twin

Your best employees have irreplaceable knowledge locked in their heads. We extract it, digitize it, and deploy it as an AI agent that anyone in your company can query — without taking a single minute of the original employee's time.

  • Ask the digital employee anything — get instant answers from their knowledge base, policies, and expertise without interrupting the real person
  • Cross-reference knowledge — have one digital employee's information interact with another's, or with a real employee's live data
  • Auto-generate outputs — spreadsheets, reports, workflows, and documentation created from digitized employee knowledge
  • Available 24/7 — the digital twin works while the real employee sleeps, travels, or focuses on high-value work
  • Train replacement employees — when an employee leaves, their digital twin becomes the training resource for their replacement, preserving institutional knowledge
  • Continuously learning — the digital employee improves as new information is added, adapting to evolving business needs

Most AI deployers race to add capabilities — more agents, more features, more integrations. We start with security. Every agent we deploy runs inside NVIDIA NemoClaw's sandboxed environment with privacy routing, policy-based access control, and full audit trails — before it touches a single piece of your data.


This isn't a checkbox. It's the foundation. Deploying AI without enterprise-grade security isn't innovation — it's liability. We build the walls first, then let the agents work inside them.

Enterprise Security & Privacy

Powered by NVIDIA NemoClaw — the enterprise security layer built for AI agents.

🛡️

Sandboxed Agent Runtime

Each AI agent runs in its own isolated environment via NVIDIA OpenShell. Agents can't access data or systems outside their defined scope.

🔐

Privacy Router

Sensitive data stays on local models running on-device. Only non-sensitive queries route to cloud models — your private data never leaves your environment.

📋

Policy-Based Access Control

Define exactly what each agent can see, execute, and report. Company-defined access policies enforced at the runtime level.

📊

Full Audit Trail

Every agent action is logged and auditable. Know exactly what your AI did, when it did it, and what data it accessed — critical for compliance.
